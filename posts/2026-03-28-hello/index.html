Hello#

I’m Ethan Choi — a Platform / DevOps Engineer with a background spanning Kubernetes operations, cloud infrastructure, and more recently, AI-driven engineering workflows.

This blog is where I write about things I’m learning, building, and thinking through.

What I’m Interested In#

My focus has shifted a lot over the years, but these days I’m most excited about:

  • Cloud & Infrastructure — multi-account governance, IaC patterns, cost optimization
  • Kubernetes — operations at scale, service mesh, observability
  • AI Tooling — agentic workflows, AI-assisted development, prompt engineering for engineers
  • Developer Productivity — the kind of tooling that makes a team 10x faster without 10x the complexity

I’ve spent the last few years working across Korean enterprise environments — Samsung, SK Telecom, SK Hynix — and now I’m building in the U.S., which has been a different kind of learning experience.

What to Expect Here#

I plan to write about:

  • Hands-on deep dives — real configs, real mistakes, real fixes
  • Architecture decisions — why I chose X over Y, and what I’d do differently
  • AI in DevOps — practical ways AI tooling is changing how I work
  • Short notes — things that took me too long to figure out, so you don’t have to

Posts will be in English. Opinions are my own.

Thanks for stopping by. More posts coming soon.
